Golden Paste

List of Ingredients

  • Lakadong Turmeric 1/2 cup
  • Raw, Unrefined, Cold-Pressed Coconut Oil 1/3 cup
      • Note Can also use:
    • linseed oil (flaxseed)
    • extra virgin olive oil
  • Fresh Black Pepper 2-3 teaspoons
  • Water 1 cup, plus more in reserve

*****************

Steps

  1. Combine turmeric and water in a saucepan and bring to a boil. Once boiling, lower heat and simmer around 7-10 minutes until it forms a thick paste. Add extra water along the way for a smooth consistency.
  2. Allow to cool for around 10 minutes (until warm to the touch but not enough to burn), thenadd the freshly cracked pepper and oil.
  3. Stir well until completely incorporated, then allow to continue to cool.
