Cheesy Bread

List of Ingredients

  • Bread Flour 3 1/2 cups
  • Water 2 cups
  • Salt 1/4 teaspoon
  • Dry Instant Yeast 1 teaspoon
  • Thickly Shredded Cheddar Cheese

Steps

  1. In a medium mixing bowl, add cool water, dry instant yeast, and salt.
  2. Add flour to the bowl. Recommend adding gently on the side of the bowl to reduce clumping.
  3. Add cheese to the bowl. Recommend adding gently to the top of the flour.
  4. Stir ingredients gently until incorporated. Recommend using the end of a spoon handle. The smaller surface area reduces how much dough sticks to the spoon
  5. First Proof: Cover with cling wrap, let sit on the counter for 8-24 hours.
  6. Place dutch oven into the oven and preheat to 450.°
  7. Second Proof: While oven preheats, prepare the second proof. Using the spoon, roll the dough in the mixing bowl once more into a ball, knocking out some of the air. Once reshaped, spray the skillet with non-stick cooking spray, and transfer dough to the skillet. It is important to use a skillet that is the same or lower diameter than the dutch oven to make the transfer easy. Cover with a lint-free towel. Allow to proof for 30 minutes.
  8. Carefully remove dutch oven from the oven, spray with non-stick cooking spray, and pour the dough out of the skillet and into the dutch oven. Gently swirl the dutch oven to reshape and center the dough. Cover and bake for 30 minutes.
  9. Uncover dutch oven and bake for 5-15 minutes more.
