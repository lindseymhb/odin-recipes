Cheesecake

List of Ingredients

Graham Cracker Crust

  • graham cracker crumbs, 1 1/2 cups
  • sugar, 2 tablespoons
  • brown (or white) sugar, 1 tablespoon
  • butter (melted), 7 tablespoons

Cheesecake

  • cream cheese (softened to room temperature), 32 oz
  • sugar, 1 cup
  • sour cream, 2/3 cups
  • vanilla extract, 1 1/2 teaspoons
  • salt, 1/8 teaspoon
  • large eggs (room temperature, lightly beaten), 4

Recommended Equipment

  • 9" springform pan
  • mixing bowls

***********************

Steps

Graham Cracker Crust

  1. Combine graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and brown sugar. Stir well.
  2. Add melted butter, and use a fork to combine ingredients well.
  3. Pour ingredients into the springform pan and press firmly into the bottom and up the sides of the pan. Set aside.

Cheesecake

  1. Preheat oven to 325.°
  2. Stir the cream cheese using a stand or hand mixer in a large bowl until smooth and creamy.
  3. Add sugar and stir again until creamy.
  4. Add sour cream, vanilla extract, and salt. Stir until well-combined.
  5. Gradually add lightly beaten eggs one at a time while the mixer stirs on low speed. After all are added, scrape the sides and mix until well combined.
  6. Pour cheesecake batter into the prepared springform pan. Place pan on cookie sheet lined with foil to protect against leaks.
  7. Transfer to center rack of the oven and bake for 50-60 minutes until edges are slightly puffed and just beginning to turn a light golden brown. The center should still be jiggly, but spring back to the touch. Don't over-bake.
  8. Remove from oven and allow to cool for 10 minutes. Then, use a night to gently loosen crust from the pan without removing the ring of the springform pan.
  9. Allow to cool for 1-2 more hours or until near room temperature before transferring to refrigerator. Allow to cool overnight (or at least 6 hours).
Note: I remove the ring of the springform pan just before serving then return it to the pan to store.
